About

JZJN is an architecture studio founded in [Year]. We work on residential, commercial, and interior projects of varying scales.

Our approach prioritizes clarity, materiality, and context. Each project begins with listening—understanding the site, the program, and the people who will inhabit the space.

We believe architecture should be straightforward. Good design emerges from careful attention to how spaces are used, how light enters a room, how materials age and weather.
